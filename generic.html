There once was a wolf.

He was born in the woods to a pack of wolves. One day he learned that a group of crows was actually called a murder of crows. He wondered why it was a murder of crows instead of a flock or pack of crows. Becauase he thought it should be a murder of wolves because wolves eat people all the time — at least in the movies they do.

Unfortunately, he was a wolf and couldn’t use google so he was plagued with that question all his life.

Did you know that a group of owls is called a “parliament”?

Neither did I.

Really, I didn’t know that. Aaaand that’s how you go down a rabbit hole on google. I know, that header image of a gecko has nothing to do with the text. It’s awfully confusing.
